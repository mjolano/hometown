WELCOME TO BACOOR CITY, CAVITE

Bacoor, Cavite, is not just where I live; it's where I grew up. The community here feels like an extended family, and the familiar faces tell the story of my upbringing. Walking around town is a stroll down memory lane, especially with places like the Aguinaldo Shrine, which holds a lot of history. Life in Bacoor isn't fancy, but it's real. The everyday sights and sounds - tricycles passing by, local fiestas, and lazy afternoons at the park - are the backdrop of my childhood. The views of Molino Dam are not just scenes; they're like old friends, always there. Bacoor, Cavite, is more than a place; it's a part of who I am. It's where I learned, played, and grew up, and those memories make it feel like home.
